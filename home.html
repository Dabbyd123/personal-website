About Me

Hi, my name is David Brocker and I am a | from New Braunfels, Tx. I began my coding journey about a year and a half ago and completely fell in love with it. Apart from taking care of my dog Winnie (more of a small horse now) and hanging out with my girlfriend Lauren, most of my free time is spent coding. If I am not coding then I am listening to coding podcasts, researching, taking online courses, etc. I have always been fascinated with the progression of technology and it's affect on our everyday lives, but I never realized that I could actually learn to code and be a part of it until recently.

I graduated from Texas State in 2016 with a degree in psychology. Web developement was not even on my radar as a potential career path while I was in school. At the time, I thought that if you were in computer science or CIS then you had pretty much been coding since you could walk. But, after talking to a few friends and family members who were in the tech industry, I realized that I was holding an antiquated view.

After college, I got a job in the healthcare field as an admissions coordniator for a hospital. I realized after about a year that I didn't have a passion for healthcare and my interests revolved more around technology. So I began taking free online classes with Codecademy, I bought a couple of courses on Udemy, and started asking a lot of questions. Since then I have learned the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript but I know there is still plenty more for me to learn. I have mainly been focused on front end devleopement and I love it, but I am also very interested in exploring back end developement.
